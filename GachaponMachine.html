How does the machine work?

By cashing in Floss Coins, you will spin the machine and receive a random floss from the stock. There are 2 tiers: tier 1, which is 3 coins and includes the entire stock, or tier 2, which is 5 coins and includes only non-dollfloss from the stock in your roll. There is no weights, which means all floss have an equal chance of being rolled. That said, you can remove all dollfloss from the roll by choosing the higher tier.

The machine is run by our staff member Lilbee! When you submit a floss for the machine, Lilbee will review it and accept it. After being acceted you'll receive a message saying that you can transfer the character to Lilbeedraws on toyhou.se. Once transfered Lilbee will give you the coins for it into your inventory.

If you roll, Lilbee will see your roll request, remove your coins and send you the floss you won on Toyhou.se!

Because the machine is rolled by a human, please be patient with us and allow at least 48 hours for a response on rolls and submission! Thank you!
