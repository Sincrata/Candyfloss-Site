How does the machine work?

By cashing in 1 Floss Coin, you will spin the machine and receive a random floss from the stock. There is no weights, which means all floss have an equal chance of being rolled.

The machine is run by our staff member Ink! When you submit a floss for the machine, Ink will review it and accept it. After being acceted you'll receive a message saying that you can transfer the character to Ink-palette on toyhou.se. Once transfered Ink will give you the coins for it into your inventory.

If you roll, Ink will see your roll request, remove your coins and send you the floss you won on Toyhou.se!

Because the machine is rolled by a human, please be patient with us and allow at least 48 hours for a response on rolls and submission! Thank you!
