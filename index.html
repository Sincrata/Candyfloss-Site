Adopt a Floss!
Check out awesome hand designed Candyfloss Characters that our STAFF have put up for sale! Check out the newest posts on toyhou.se or in the discord!

Complete Achievements to earn badges and Floss Coins that can be used to purchase MYOs or spent for a random floss in the Gachapon! You can gift art to the Floss of the Month without owning a floss to begin with!

Floss of the Month

Chase is this month's Floss of the month!

Chase is a Thetan Peppermintfloss who loves the night life! Parties, bright lights, and huge crowds are his happy place!
He's a party animal that can't be stopped! He's a rambunctious and energetic demiboy who just wants to have a good time and let loose!! He is a pop idol from the land of Phi and thrives on party culture and fame!
Chase comes from a poor background, and has worked hard to become the star he is today! He is known to use his invisibility in his concerts to make it appear as if he pops in and out of different locations while he sings.


Chase belongs to the user Ghosting30s! Ghosting is a long standing member of the Candyfloss Community and is always bringing smiles and good vibes wherever it goes!
It is always around to support others and joke around, bringing smiles to everyone's faces!
Thank you for being a part of our community, Ghosting!
