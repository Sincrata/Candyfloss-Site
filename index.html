Get Your Own Floss!

Want to get a Candyfloss of your very own?!? There are many ways to get your hands on a floss! Here are some easy options!
 
MYO Raffles!
Every month we have a free to enter MYO raffle in the discord! Join the dicord and head over to the "MYO-raffles" Channel to throw your hat in the ring!
Adopt a Floss!
Check out awesome hand designed Candyfloss Characters that our STAFF have put up for sale! Check out the newest posts on toyhou.se or in the discord!
Earn Floss Coins!
Complete Achievements to earn badges and Floss Coins that can be used to purchase MYOs or spent for a random floss in the Gachapon! You can gift art to the Floss of the Month without owning a floss to begin with!
Buy an MYO ticket!
You can always purchase MYO tickets to Make your Own floss at any point! Check out the options at the MYO center and submit them for approval in the Discord server!

Pool Floaties Chain Game!

Splash into our summer time chain game!
Dive in to the chain and draw the Candyfloss before you beating the heat and catching some rays in the pool. Whether they are blasting water guns or lounging on a giant flamingo floatie, the choice is yours! Just depict a Floss floating their worries away, and the next artist will draw yours! Pass the torch, give a gift, and get in on the sunny poolside fun!

Love Arrow Story Prompt

Cupidfloss are in the air looking to give some unsuspecting Floss a love bug!
But their aim isn't always straight. What happens when a rogue love arrow strikes your Candyfloss right in the heart!? Will this sudden accident lead to absolute chaos, or is it the start of a beautiful romance? Write a story from the perspective of a cupidfloss (can be one of your floss or an npc) or from the perspective of the sudden lovers!

Floss of the Month

Medha is this month's Floss of the month!

She's a Sourfloss student living in the Epsilon region.
She's a bright and social floss who cares a lot about philosophy and ethics. Never fully identifying with the technology centric culture of the Sigma region, she moved to the Epsilon region to be more in touch with her epsilon telepathic abilities.
She's currently living in with Rosemary, a sweet old Honeyfloss librarian that was her mother's college professor back in the day!


Medha belongs to JubLubDubs! JubLubs is a recent member of the Candyfloss community, but she's already been a delight to be around!
She has a very fun and unique art style that's always nice to see in the art channels! We're glad you've been enjoying Candyfloss, both in lore and the community!
Thank you for being a part of Candyfloss, JubLub!
