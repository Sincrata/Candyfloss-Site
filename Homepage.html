Get Your Own Floss!

Want to get a Candyfloss of your very own?!? There are many ways to get your hands on a floss! Here are some easy options!
 
MYO Raffles!
Every month we have a freee to enter MYO raffle in the discord! Join the dicord and head over to the "MYO-raffles" Channel to throw your hat in the ring!
Adopt a Floss!
Check out awesome hand designed Candyfloss Characters that our STAFF have put up for sale! Check out the newest posts on toyhou.se or in the discord!
Prompt Coins!
Join in on one of the prompts this month to earn Floss Coins that can be used to purchase MYOs or spent for a random floss in the Gachapon! You can gift art to the Floss of the Month without owning a floss to begin with!
Buy an MYO ticket!
You can always purchase MYO tickets to Make your Own floss at any point! Check out the options at the MYO centerand submit them for approval in the Discord server!

Epsilon

The Epsilon Clan is the spiritual clan. It is made up of a modern monk-like people who put great stock into their physical, mental, and emotional well being. Religion is an ever present part of the Epsilon culture and even those who do not believe in higher power tend to take on many of the religious practices like feng shui, meditation and spiritual centering that the Epsilon believe in for mental and physical health purposes. Many meditate for hours at a time and all strive to find inner peace. Epsilons put very little importance on material things and instead advocate for kindness and patience above all else.

Read more about the Epsilon Clan!and look out for Epsilon discounts across the site this month, like clothing, bases, and petpets found in this clan!
